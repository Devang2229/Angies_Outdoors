Angie's Outdoor, Barrie, Ontario, is the outdoor clothing Store.Angie's Outdoor is the place to visit in Barrie and central Ontario for the largest selection of men and women outdoor clothing. It is worth the trip from Toronto, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Newmarket, Aurora, Vaughan and anywhere in Simcoe County to see what Angie's Outdoor has to offer. We carry parkas, down-filled garments, vests, fleece, pants, waterproof and breathable clothing and casual wear. Undergarments for layering including moisture-wicking and merino wool garments will keep you comfortable. Hats, toques, ear-muffs and t-necks as well as gloves, mittens and quick dry clothing are all available. Top brands like Sherpa, Woolpower, Stanfield, Polar Therm, Kombi and Far West and friendly service will ensure you are well clothed for your outdoor adventure. Whether you are skiing, snowboarding, hiking or just enjoying the outdoors-Angie's staff will serve you knowledgeably and cheerfully.

Angie's Outdoor also carries a surplus of military clothing. Come to the outdoor store in Central Ontario, GTA camping and outdoor equipment outfitter for all your outdoor clothing needs.
