Profile for Angie's Outdoors



Angie's Outdoor is the place to visit in Barrie and central Ontario for the largest selection of men's and women's winter and summer outdoor clothing. We carry parkas, down-filled garments, vests, fleece, pants, waterproof and breathable clothing and casual wear.
Undergarments for layering including moisture-wicking and merino wool garments will keep you comfortable. Hats, toques, ear-muffs and t-necks as well as gloves, mittens and quick dry clothing are all available. Top brands like Sherpa, Canada Goose, Stanfield, Polar Therm, Kombi and Far West and friendly service will ensure you are well clothed for your outdoor adventure. Whether you are skiing, snowboarding, hiking, camping or just enjoying the outdoors-Angie's staff will serve you knowledgeably and cheerfully.
